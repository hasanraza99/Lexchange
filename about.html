Lexchange began in 2023 when a group of language enthusiasts wanted to create a platform focused on authentic conversation practice without the barriers found on other platforms. We noticed that many existing language exchange platforms were either cluttered with ads, had poor matching systems, or lacked important safety features.

Our team has personal experience with the challenges of language learning, having collectively studied over 15 different languages. We understand firsthand how crucial regular conversation practice is for fluency, and we're committed to making that accessible to everyone.
